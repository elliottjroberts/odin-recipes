Beans On Toast

Description:

The ultimate speedy dinner. (Or lunch, or breakfast...)

  • A tin of beans.
  • A slice of bread.
  • A parent to help, if you don't know how to use the microwave.

Steps to follow:

  1. Place your bread in the toaster.
  2. Put beans in the microwave.
  3. Place the beans on the toast.
  4. Voila, beans on the toast.
